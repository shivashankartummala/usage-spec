# Standardization Roadmap

## Phase 0: Draft Stabilization
- Freeze v1 core syscall semantics.
- Publish lifecycle and threat model baselines.

## Phase 1: Multi-Implementation Validation
- Validate against at least two independent substrates.
- Publish conformance matrix and known deviations.

## Phase 2: CNCF Community Review
- Open design review with TAG Runtime and TAG Security.
- Iterate on semantic conventions and policy vocabulary.

## Phase 3: Beta Specification
- Versioned profiles and backward compatibility policy.
- Interop plugfest and certification criteria.

## Phase 4: GA Specification
- Ratified conformance suite.
- Long-term governance and extension process.
